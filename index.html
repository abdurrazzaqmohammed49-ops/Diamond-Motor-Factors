Our Story

Built on a Passion
for the Motor Trade

Diamond Motor Factors was founded in 2005 by Declan Diamond, a Dublin native with over a decade of experience in automotive engineering and the parts trade. Frustrated by poor service and overpriced parts from large chains, Declan set out to create something different.

Starting from a small unit in Dublin 12 with a team of three, Diamond Motor Factors quickly earned a reputation for stocking hard-to-find parts, offering genuine expert advice, and treating every customer with respect — whether a sole trader mechanic or a large fleet operator.

Today, we operate from a 15,000 sq ft facility at Parkwest Industrial Estate, employ 22 specialists, and maintain relationships with over 300 parts suppliers worldwide. We're proud to still be independently owned, still answering the phone with honesty and expertise.

Our Journey

20 Years in the Making

2005

Founded in Dublin

Diamond Motor Factors opened its doors in Dublin 12, serving local garages and drivers with quality parts at fair prices.

2009

Launched our dedicated trade division, partnering with over 100 garages across greater Dublin with preferential pricing.

2013

Warehouse Expansion

Moved to our current 15,000 sq ft facility at Parkwest Industrial Estate, tripling stock capacity to 30,000+ parts.
